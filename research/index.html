Research Interestes

Pandemic impact on young Canadians with chronic health conditions

In February 2021 I began my postdoc. I work on a large observational multi-wave survey study on COVID-19 impacts. Presently I am analyzing quasi-representative samples from Canada and evaluating the psychological impact of the pandemic on young Canadians with and without chronic health conditions.

I am patiently waiting for feedback before I post anything more :)

Fertility preservation in young people with cancer in Europe

I have started collaborating with Youth Cancer Europe in 2019. I help with patient advocacy and research agenda, and work as a scientific liaison. We just launched a pan-European study on Fertility preservation services.

Cancer prevention & European Cancer Leagues (ECL)

I became a Youth Ambassador for ECL in April 2019. I am currently heading the research working group. More to follow soon.
